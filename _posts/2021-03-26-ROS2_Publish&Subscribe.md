COMMANDS TO USE:
- ros2 topic list
- ros2 msg list
- ros2 msg show std_msgs/String
- ros2 topic pub /chatter std_msgs/String "data: Hello ROS Developers"
- ros2 topic echo /chatter


it is quite similar to ROS1

- ros2 topic pub <topic_name> <msg> <structure>
